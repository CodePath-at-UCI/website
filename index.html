About CodePath

Welcome to CodePath at UCI's website!
CodePath is a national non-profit that exits to provide the tools and connections that software engineers need to unlock their greatest career potential. Our free, high quality courses are taught by trained UCI students, using curriculum created by CodePath.
On this site, you'll find information about our upcoming courses as well as resources to help you better understand the material!
